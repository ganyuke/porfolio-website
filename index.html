Signal is a free and open-source end-to-end encrypted instant messaging service available on Android, iOS, and PC and used by journalists and world leaders across the globe, funded not by ads, but by donations to the 501(c)(3) nonprofit Signal Foundation.

Signal prides itself on being a private competitor to Whatsapp; one of its founders even being Whatsapp's former co-founder.

The Signal Protocol has been independently audited by security researchers across the world, passing with flying colors. The app has also been endorsed by the famed (or perhaps notorious) NSA whistleblower himself: Edward Snowden.

As of January 2021, Signal has been downloaded over 105 million times, boasting 40 million concurrent users.
